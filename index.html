About

The South West Regional 12 Bell Competition committee is now inviting entries for the 2024 competition. The 2024 competition will take place at Withycome Raleigh on Saturday 12th October with the draw to take place at 10am.

Any twelve-bell tower, band or Association that rings regularly on twelve bells within the counties of Gloucestershire, Bristol, Wiltshire, Somerset, Dorset, Devon and Cornwall, is invited to enter a team.

The touch this year will be 264 Grandsire Cinques as per this calling.

If you would like to enter, or would like to have an informal chat, please contact the committee here.

Competition entries should be sent to Southwest 12 Bell.

Entries should include the following:

  1. Confirmation that your team is planning to attend

  2. The provisional list of ringers (this is for Health and Safety on the day and can be changed nearer the time)

  3. Provide a name and contact details for your team captain
