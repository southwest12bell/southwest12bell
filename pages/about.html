Images

The images in the banner are of some of the 12 bell towers in the South West. From left to right they are: Chilcompton (Somerset), Cirencester (Gloucestershire), Crediton (Devon), Exeter Cathedral (Devon), Midsomer Norton (Somerset), St Mary Redcliffe (Bristol), South Petherton (Somerset), Taunton (Somerset), Trowbridge (Wiltshire) and Truro Cathedral (Cornwall).
